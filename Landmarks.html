Famous Landmarks Around the World

Here are the 6 Notable Landmarks known around the world!!!

Eiffel Tower

 

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world, located in Paris, France.

It was built in 1889 and stands at 324 meters tall, attracting millions of visitors every year.

The tower lights up beautifully at night, making it a magical sight to behold.

Great Wall of China

 

The Great Wall of China is an ancient fortification built to protect Chinese states from invasions.

Stretching over 13,000 miles, it is one of the longest structures ever built by humans.

Visitors can explore different sections of the wall, each offering stunning views and history.

Statue of Liberty

 

Located in New York City, the Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom and democracy.

It was a gift from France to the United States in 1886 and stands at 93 meters tall.

Visitors can climb to the top for breathtaking views of the city.

Pyramids of Giza

 

The Pyramids of Giza in Egypt are some of the most famous ancient structures in the world.

Built over 4,500 years ago, they were tombs for the pharaohs and remain a great mystery.

The Great Pyramid is the largest, standing at 146 meters high.

Colosseum

 

The Colosseum in Rome, Italy, is an ancient amphitheater built for gladiator battles.

It could hold up to 50,000 spectators and remains one of the most visited attractions in Italy.

Despite its age, much of its structure still stands today, offering a glimpse into ancient Roman culture.

Machu Picchu

 

Machu Picchu in Peru is a breathtaking Incan city built high in the Andes Mountains.

It was rediscovered in 1911 and is now one of the most famous archaeological sites in the world.

The site offers stunning views, and visitors can hike up to experience its beauty firsthand.
